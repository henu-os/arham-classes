OUR STORY

Built on Trust & Excellence

Arham Coaching Classes was founded by Monika Jain with a simple yet powerful mission — to make quality education accessible to every student in Bhilwara, regardless of their background or starting point.

With over two decades of teaching experience, Monika Ma'am identified a critical gap: students were studying hard but struggling to score — not from lack of effort, but from lack of conceptual clarity.

Arham Coaching Classes was built to bridge that gap. Through small batches, regular testing, and a deeply personalized approach, we've helped hundreds of students achieve 90%+ scores and build the confidence that carries them through life.

"Learning Beyond Books — we build people, not just scorecards."
